Security, Verification & Trust

Secure Payments

All payments flow through secure escrow. Employers fund tasks upfront; payments are released after approval to protect both parties.

Identity Verification

To prevent fraud and ensure payment compliance, workers complete a one-time KYC using a trusted provider. Only minimal information is needed.

Quality Controls

Automated checks and human spot checks maintain quality. Dispute resolution and appeal processes are available for rejected work.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?
Payments are released after approval. You can withdraw to PayPal, Payoneer, bank transfer or supported crypto wallets. Typical processing time: 1‚Äì3 business days (varies by method).
Are tasks legitimate?
Yes. We vet employers and run automated checks to prevent fraudulent campaigns. If a task is suspicious, report it to support immediately.
Can I work from any country?
Most countries are supported, but some tasks are geo-restricted due to legal or survey provider rules. Check task details before starting.
What if my work is rejected?
You can appeal rejections via the dispute process. We provide evidence & feedback. Repeated low-quality submissions may lead to temporary holds.
